Yoga: The Source of Chitta, Vridhi, and Nirodhaha

Published on July 08, 2025

Yoga serves as the primal source of the mind’s fluctuations (chitta), its growth (vridhi), and its restraint (nirodhaha). Through disciplined practice, one cultivates focus, expands awareness, and learns to settle thoughts.
